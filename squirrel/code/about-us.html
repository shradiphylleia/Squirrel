About Us



Welcome to our platform dedicated to promoting the use of native seeds!
We believe in the importance of preserving biodiversity and supporting
local ecosystems by encouraging the use of native plant species.

Our platform serves as a hub for individuals and projects seeking to source and donate native seeds within their vicinity.

Mission!



Our mission is to promote the use of native seeds in landscaping,
agriculture, and ecosystem restoration projects. By facilitating
the donation and exchange of native seeds, we aim to increase the
availability of these seeds and encourage their use in a wide range
of projects, from backyard gardens to large-scale restoration efforts.

How?



Users can create an account on our platform and browse through
listings of available native seeds in their area.They can also list
seeds that they have available for donation. Once a match is found,users
can connect with each other to arrange the donation or exchange.

Why?



Native seeds are essential for maintaining biodiversity
and supporting local ecosystems. They are adapted to local
climate conditions and provide important habitat and food
sources for native wildlife. By promoting the use of native
seeds, we can help restore and preserve the natural beauty and
ecological health of our communities.

Join Us!



Whether you are a gardener, a farmer, or a conservationist,
you can make a difference by donating or exchanging native
seeds through our platform. Join us in promoting the use of
native seeds and help us create a more sustainable future for
our planet.
